Hi! My name is Heather Hansen. I have been married for 16 years and have 4 awesome children. I am a stay-at-home mom and my family's #1 cheerleader. They are my world. Several years ago, my kids wanted me to color with them, but my daughter had a very specific image she wanted to color. I searched for images in some coloring books and on the internet. We did not find what she was looking for, so i decided to draw it for her. After I completed her image, I just kept drawing and learned that I was pretty good at it. I have continued to draw and build up my portfolio of clipart images and coloring pages.
